Strategies to win tic-tac-toe: 1.Play your first X in a corner. 2.Try to win if your opponent plays the first O in the center. 3.Win automatically if your opponent plays his first O in any square besides the center. 4.Place your third X so you have two possible winning moves. 5.Win with your fourth X. etc..
